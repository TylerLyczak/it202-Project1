About

Our company starts with rich roots.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The security platform was made by Tyler Lyczak, an aspiring programmer at the University of Illinois at Chicago. It was his main project and with him coding it himself, it was challenging.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    As the project got more serious, Tyler reached out to many programmers online to help him. Since new viruses were being released everyday, having a team made patching fairly easy. From there, the company was started with many user joining. They decided to name the company T&L Security.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    As the team assembled under a name, they released it to the public with great reception. People loved the customizability of the software, making it gain a lot of popularity.

  • July 2014

    Phase Two Expansion

    As T&L Security started to expand and gain many users, the team also expanded. With this expansion, we saw many new features added to the program, like a disk cleaner and cookie cleaner.

  • December 2017

    Where We Stand Today

    T&L Security grew so much over the years. We were able to add a data storage warehouse, allowing our user to backup data while keeping it very secured. Companies, such as Norton Security, sought out to buy T&L Security. Instead of this, we want to dedicate our service to our customers because they see something in us that other companies do not have. We appreciate it.
